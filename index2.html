About Luminary

Welcome to Luminary - Your Career Guidance Platform

Luminary is a comprehensive educational website designed to help students find their perfect career path based on their interests and abilities. We believe in "Turning Aspiration into Ability" by providing the right guidance and resources at every step of your educational journey.

Career Guidance

Choose the right career path based on your interests, strengths, and academic performance.

Academic Support

Get guidance on subject selection from 10th grade through degree programs.

Knowledge Enhancement

Develop extra knowledge in English grammar, mathematical tricks, mythological stories, and more.

Step-by-Step Planning

Clear roadmap from school to college to career with actionable steps.

How Luminary Helps You:
  • 10th Grade: Choose subjects based on your interests and career aspirations
  • 12th Grade: Select the right stream and plan for higher education
  • Degree Programs: Get guidance on choosing the right course and college
  • Skill Development: Enhance your knowledge beyond academics
  • Resource Access: Get recommended books and study materials
  • Expert Connect: Access contact information of popular teachers

Luminary makes your educational journey easy and focused by providing personalized guidance at every stage. Whether you're confused about subject selection, stream choice, or career options, we're here to illuminate your path!
